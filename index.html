A game made just for fun.

What is it

It is a game where you attack a zombie fortress but the zombies attack you too.

How do you play

You have to train troops and you can place them anywhere like clash of clans. But there will be enemies attacking you too while you are invading so you must put in defences. It has a few charaters from games and movies. you would have to destroy the fortress in order to win. If the zombies attacks you, you lose

Types of troops

There is ranged, close quaters, far attack and defensive

What else is in there

There is different rewards and currency. For rewards it could just be level up, a new kind of troop or treasure

Gamemodes

There is normal mode, mutiplayer mode, where you can play against your friends and it would be harder

What kind of troops are there

There is military, ninjas, barbarians, superheroes, plants, explosive weapons, monsters and science fiction
